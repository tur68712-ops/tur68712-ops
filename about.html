About Me

I am a fourth year student majoring in biology in the premed track.I chose this major because ever since high school biology courses always drew my attention, it was the one subject where I felt at home in a way. One of my favorites have been biochemistry as it was taught by an amazing professor and the content of the course was extremely interesting and though provoking.

With most of the courses I have taken in my college career have been heavy on science and math, I didn’t really have much experience coming into the course. However, I can now say that I can confidently and comfortably use GIS to conduct data analysis and make maps that look good. Apart from being everything biology I really enjoy reading (one of my favorites being Charlotte's web.)and doing art, I mostly use acrylic paints or graphite in my works and have been lucky enough to be able to sell some of my works internationally.
